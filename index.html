For Patients

Inservio was established in 2007 when intravitreal injection treatment options for patients by Ophthalmologists required special handling and now represents the gold standard for cold chain medication management to ophthalmology clinics.

Inservio acts as the agent for the patient arranging the medication to be dispensed by a pharmacy and delivered to your clinic in time for your appointment.

Our service ensures that you do not need to collect your medication from a local pharmacy, and you do not have to worry about transporting the refrigerated medications to your appointment.
